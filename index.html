This research compares Wikipedia and Grokipedia (version 0.1, accessed 1 November 2025 with 885,279 articles) on topics where both platforms have coverage, focusing on content quality. Analyzed across technical domains (blockchain, space tech, AI, entrepreneurship), the study reveals that AI-generated encyclopedias can match human-curated quality on accuracy while excelling in timeliness and citation breadth.
